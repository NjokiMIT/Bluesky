ABOUT BLUESKY

We Build for Your Comfort

Bluesky Maintenance Services is a company that works towards making your home as heavenly as possible. Have you been looking for a trusted partner you can trust with the project of renovating your home, well, look no more. We make it our sole purpose to serve you and make sure that we fulfill that which you dream of. Bluesky Services were first initiated in the year 2020 with an aim of meeting Kenya's Affordable Housing Agenda by the year 2030. We have clients all over the country and even in the East African Region. We have been tasked by the Government in undertaking various project all over the country.
